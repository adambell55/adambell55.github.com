About Me

Hi, I’m Adam Bell. I’ve always been deeply fascinated by the journey of personal growth, having spent the last 30 years helping people, teams, and organisations grow themselves through leadership, coaching, and strategic development — both in the private and non-profit sectors.


As a student of self -development, I am passionate about building and maintaining a growth mindset, living intentionally, and consistently doing the inner work I need to do to maintain a positive and prodcutive mindest, even through tough and challenging times. Focussing on the application of what I was learning and taking the necessary actions, has helped me radically change my mindset for the better. I’m happier and calmer; I am more patient; I have greater resiliency, deeper focus, stronger determination, and greater faith and confidence than I ever have had.


So, as a way to help everyday people like myself, to create their own lasting change, I created MasterMinds: The Ultimate Mindset Transformation Journal as a way to make timeless wisdom accessible, practical, affordable, personal, and actionable - no hype, no shortcuts, just meaningful, sustainable, real-world growth. When I’m not writing or trying to make people laugh, I work in healthcare and love nothing more than spending quality time with my family and doing my best everday living by the principles I’m sharing with you.
